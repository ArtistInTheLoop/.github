# CreatorsInTheLoop Games

We make games with AI and humans together!

Our games are designed using a proprietary pipeline that combines stochastic simulation and AI-driven design at scale. Thousands of games are generated, simulated and stress-tested. The ones that survive are mechanically sharp, balanced, replayable and deep where it matters.

Then the creative work begins. Every card is drawn/painted by an artist. All written material is reviewed and refined by a writer. Our creative decisions are made with intention, not automation.

In a world racing to remove people from the creative process, we've got a pipeline that puts them at the centre of it.

The result: games that play like they were designed by a team of fifty and look like they were made by a few people... because they were!
